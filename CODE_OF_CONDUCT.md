# Code of Conduct

We aim to provide a welcoming, inclusive, and harassmentâ€‘free environment for all contributors and users of MaterialDerailleur.

## Our standards
- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Assume good intent and seek clarification before escalating.
- Give constructive, actionable feedback.
- Respect people's privacy and do not share personal data without consent.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or hateful language.
- Doxxing, threats, or sharing private/personal information.
- Sustained disruption of project activities or abusive conduct.

## Reporting & enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
1. Contact a project maintainer directly via GitHub private issue or email (maintainers listed in README).
2. Provide as much information as possible (date, time, participants, links, and screenshots if applicable).
3. Maintainers will acknowledge receipt and follow up within a reasonable time frame.
4. The maintainers may take actions including warnings, blocking, or involving platform support, depending on severity.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, chat channels) and public events.

## Acknowledgements
This project is committed to creating a supportive community. Thank you for helping us maintain a positive environment.

For legal or high-severity issues, contact the project lead directly or GitHub support.
